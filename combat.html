Actions

Every turn you may take one action and one half action.

You may freely exchange one action for two half actions and vice versa.

Attack

Melee

Ranged

Move

Move 6 paces.

Recover

Regain 1d6 + Constitution hit points, but you need to make a Constitution save or gain a stack of Exhaustion.

Half Actions

Sprint

Move 1d6 paces.

Reactions

Momentum

When you win a contest versus an opponent, you gain a stack of momentum.

Each stack of momentum grants you a +2 bonus to any combat related rolls.

You can normally have up to 3 stacks of momentum.

If you lose a contest, you lose all stacks of momentum.
