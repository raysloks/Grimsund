Rolling vs Picking

I would argue that rolling is superior, but you have my fullest support if you choose to pick instead.

Stats

For each of your character's seven primary stats, roll 3d6 and use the middle value as your stat.

Background

Roll or select a background appropriate for your race. Relevant tables can be found on your race's page.

This will determine your starting career.

Roll once on the career's stat advancement table.

Gain the career's free advancements.

Select one of the career's available perks.

Gain the career's Equipment.

Skills

Gain one rank in a number of skills equal to 5 + Intelligence.

Connections

Randomly determine two of your party members.

For each of them, roll on this table to determine how the two of you know each other.

Note that any 'are' can be replaced by 'were' at your behest.
