Rolling vs Picking

Stats

For each of your character's seven primary stats, roll 3d6 and use the middle value as your stat.

Race

Pick a race or roll on this table.

d20Race
1-15Human
16Kobold
17Goblin
18Orc
19Dwarf
20Elf

Adjust your stats based on your race. Stats labeled as 'Half' or 'Third' are rounded up.

Background

Roll or select a background appropriate for your race.

This will determine your starting career.

Gain the career's free advancements.

Roll once on the career's stat advancement table.

Roll 5 + Intelligence times on the career's skill advancement table.

Gain the career's Equipment.

Origin

Roll or select an area from which you hail.

Gain the skill Local Knowledge for that area.
