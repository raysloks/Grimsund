Perks

General

These perks will come in handy in all sorts of situations.

Well Traveled

Gain a +2 bonus to all Local Knowledge tests.

Second Wind

Once per short rest, you may lose one level of exhaustion.

Fleet

When using a movement action you may move an additional pace.

Sprinter

When using the Sprint action, you may move an additional 1d6 paces.

Disappearing Act

When trying to escape someone or something following you, you gain a +3 bonus on relevant tests.

Adrenaline Rush

You may gain +2 Strength and Agility for 1 minute. Then gain 2 levels of exhaustion.

Disease Resistant

Gain a +4 bonus on saves against diseases.

Old Man and the Sea

Gain a +2 bonus to Wisdom if you have seen the sea since you last slept.

Of the People

Gain a +3 bonus on social tests when interacting with low-born humans.

I Had Me Fooled

You may make a Deception (Charisma) test to make yourself believe a fact.

The DC of the test is 15 + Intelligence. The referee may raise the DC if believing the fact would be improbable.

You may not attempt multiple tests for the same fact.

If you are provided proof that the fact is false you must make another test to keep believing.

Cold Resistant

Heat Resistant

Combat

These perks will aid you in combat, but will find little use outside of it.

Intercept

You may use a reaction to move into the way of an attack. Move 1d6 + 5 paces, as per Sprint. If you manage to move in front of the target or adjacent to the attacker, you become the target of the attack.

A Moment's Respite

When you Recover, you regain 1d6 additional hit points.

Press the Attack

Whenever you gain a 3rd stack of momentum, you may instantly make an attack.

Turn the Tide

When you attack a target with 3 or more stacks of momentum, you make the attack with advantage.

Shield Expert

Gain +2 Melee and Ranged defense when using a shield.

Might Makes Right

Deal 1 extra damage with melee attacks.

Cleave

When you hit a target with a melee attack, you may instantly make an attack against a target that is adjacent to both you and the original target.

Tough

Gain 3 total hit points.

Brawler

Gain a +2 bonus to Melee when unarmed.

Gain a +2 bonus to Melee and Strength when grappling or being grappled.

Sharpshooter

When making a ranged attack, the penalty due to range is halved.

Riposte

When an enemy misses a melee attack against you, you may make an attack of opportunity against them.

You may only use Riposte once per round.

Strike When the Time is Right

Gain a +2 to attacks of opportunity.

Point Blank Shot

Making a ranged attack does not provoke an attack of opportunity from the target of the attack.

Bloodlust

Hitting an attack restores 1 hit point.

Decimate

Your attacks deal 1 additional damage per 10 total hit points the target has.

Warforged

Gain 50% bonus armor.

True Survivor

If you are at full hit points, you cannot be reduced to 0 hit points by a single attack.

Arrow-breaker

Reduces damage taken from ranged sources by 1.

Rapid Fire
