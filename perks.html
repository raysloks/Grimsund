Perks

Below are all the perks a 1st level character can take.

General

These perks will come in handy in all sorts of situations.

Well Traveled

Count as having an extra rank in all Local Knowledge tests, even those you have no ranks in.

Second Wind

Once per short rest, you may recover two levels of exhaustion.

Fleet

When using a movement action you may move an additional pace.

Sprinter

When using the Sprint action, you may move an additional 1d6 paces.

Disappearing Act

When trying to escape someone or something following you, you make relevant non-combat tests with advantage.

Disease Resistant

Make saves against diseases with advantage.

Old Man and the Sea

When making willpower, resolve, and morale tests, replace any die result less than 10 with 10.

Improvisation

Reduces the penalty to untrained skills by 3.

I Had Me Fooled

You may make a Deception (Charisma) test to make yourself believe a fact.

The DC of the test is 15 + Intelligence. The referee may raise the DC if believing the fact would be improbable.

You may not attempt multiple tests for the same fact.

If you are provided proof that the fact is false you must make another test to keep believing.

Cold Resistant

Make saves against cold weather effects with advantage.

Reduce cold damage taken by 1.

Heat Resistant

Make saves against hot weather effects with advantage.

Reduce fire damage taken by 1.

Combat

These perks will aid you in combat, but will find little use outside of it.

Intercept

Reaction

You move to intercept an attack. Move 1d6 + 5 paces, as per Sprint. If you manage to move in front of the target or adjacent to the attacker, you become the target of the attack.

A Moment's Respite

When you Recover, you regain 1d6 additional hit points.

Press the Attack

Whenever you gain a 3rd stack of momentum, you may instantly make a Strike.

Turn the Tide

Whenever an enemy adjactent to you gains a 3rd stack of momentum, you may instantly make a Strike against them.

This strike ignores any bonuses and penalties granted by momentum.

Might Makes Right

Strikes deal 1 extra damage.

Cleave

When you hit a target with a Melee Attack; Charge; or other attack action, you may instantly make a Strike against a target that is adjacent to both you and the original target.

Tough

Gain 3 total hit points.

Gain a +1 bonus to Constitution saves.

Brawler

Gain a +2 bonus to Melee when unarmed.

Gain a +2 bonus to Melee while grappling or being grappled.

Gain a +2 bonus to all combat manoeuvers.

Sharpshooter

When making a Shot, the penalty due to range is halved.

Riposte

When an enemy misses a Strike against you, you may make an Opportunity Attack against them.

Opportunity Attacks do not trigger Riposte.

Strike When the Time is Right

When holding your turn, you may resume it after making an Opportunity Attack.

This effectively puts the turn of whose ever turn it was on hold until you have ended your turn.

Point Blank Shot

Ranged Attack does not allow an Opportunity Attack from the target.

Bloodlust

Hitting a Strike restores 1 hit point.

Decimate

Your attacks deal 1 additional damage per 10 total hit points the target has.

Warforged

Gain 50% bonus armor.

Iron Man

If the damage you take is reduced to 0 or below, you suffer no damage instead of the usual 1.

True Survivor

At the start of your turn, if you have less than 5 hit points remaining, you regain 1 hit point.

Rapid Fire

Action

Make two Shots against a target in range. Both shots are made with disadvantage.

Allows Opportunity Attacks.

The expected damage output is larger than a Ranged Attack when the chance to hit is larger than 50%.

Go! Go! Go!

Action

Grant an adjacent ally 2d6 Initiative.

Tenacious

Every stun effect you suffer can be mitigated by a Constitution save at the end of your turn, even if you normally would not be allowed one.

If the effect does not have a DC associated with it, the save's DC is 20.

Massacre

Reducing an enemy to 0 hit points allows you to immediately take an action.

Massacre can only trigger once per turn.
