Activities

A downtime period is divided into weeks.

Characters spend their weeks performing various activities.

You may be able to perform downtime activities in shorter time spans, but you will probably have to return to them later in order to reap the benefits.

Subsisting

The minimum coinage needed to survive without working for a week is usually about 5 c.

Carousing

Frivously spending your hard-earned coins to gain a small amount of experience.

Every 10 c you spend grants you 1 xp.

You may gain up to 4 xp through carousing in a single downtime period. After which you must go on another adventure before you can gain any more.
