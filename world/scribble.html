So how does magic work?

Like, that's a pretty big question.

I want to have that feel of GREEN, ADVENTURE, WEIRD AND ODD BUT NOT TOO MUCH.

What exactly does that mean?

Titans

I'm okay with titans as an origin for magic and what not.

It's sort of like the "the gods are the source of all magic" but at the same time, titans don't have the same connotations as gods, so i feel like i can do more of the things i want to do.

I struggle with striking a balance between "science magic" and "handwave magic". Dunno exactly how descriptive these terms are. But I think they get my feelings across decently.

So the titans left behind... what?

Remnants

Description

What are they? Many different things. Wow, that's very specific.

Would it help if i designed like 6 different titans? So i could then more easily imagine parts of them specifically being left behind?

Function

What do they do? You guessed it! Many. Different. Things.

However, if you got one lying around in a forest it's gonna have an effect on the environment.

Like a drop of burning titan blood is probably gonna set fire to the forest first of all.

If it landed in a lake, that lake might just become a giant steam vent or something. Maybe get fish "swimming" around in the steam, driven mad by the titan's influence.
