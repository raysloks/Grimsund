CostEffect
BaseHeavily oxidizes the surface of a fist-sized piece of iron you touch over the course of a few seconds. Alloys will only suffer small spots of surface level rust.
+1Allows you to oxidize alloys as well, although they must be predominantly iron.
+1Allows the rust to fully permeate the item.
+1Double the amount of metal that can be affected, as well as the time spent oxidizing it.
+1You can spread the effect over multiple targets.
+1You don't need to continue touching the target, although you will still need to concentrate for the duration.
+1The spell's range is increased by 1d6 paces.
