Secondary

These stats are derived from your primary stats, your perks, and your equipment.

Hit Points

Your base hit point total is equal to:

5 + Constitution

Armor

Every point of Armor reduces the raw damage you take by 1.

Melee

Your base Melee Offense is equal to:

(Strength * 2 + Dexterity) / 3

Your base Melee Defense is equal to:

10 + (Agility * 2 + Dexterity) / 3

These are rounded to the nearest integer.

Unarmed

If you are unarmed, you have a -6 penalty to Melee.

Ranged

Your base Ranged Offense bonus is equal to your Dexterity.

This is most certainly gonna be changed to something else.

Skills

You can normally put up to 4 ranks into a skill.

The first rank allows you to make relevant Skill checks without a -6 penalty.

Each subsequent rank grants a +2 bonus.

Gameplay

Rolling Dice

Tests

Roll a d20 and add any relevant modifiers.

A test may use a stat; a skill; both; or neither as modifiers.

If the result is larger than or equal to the check's Difficulty Class, you succeed at the test.

An average check has a DC of 16.

Tests are sometimes referred to as checks or saves.

Contests

Roll a d20 and add any relevant modifiers.

Your opponent does the same.

Whoever has the largest result wins.

If both results are the same, nothing happens most of the time.

Examples

Leveling Up

  • Increase one Primary Stat by 1.
  • Increase Hit Points by 1.
  • Gain one perk.
  • Gain one skill rank.

You cannot advance a primary stat past 9. Racial modifiers affect this number; for example: an orc can advance their Strength all the way to 12, but their Dexterity is limited to 6.
