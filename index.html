Your base Melee defense bonus is equal to:

(Agility * 2 + Dexterity) / 3

Skills

You can normally put up to 3 ranks into a skill.

The first rank allows you to make relevant Skill checks without a -6 penalty.

Each subsequent rank grants a +2 bonus.

Gameplay

Tests

Roll a d20 and add any relevant modifiers.

If the result is larger than or equal to the check's Difficulty Class, you succeed at the test.

An average check has a DC of 16.

Tests are sometimes referred to as checks or saves.

Contests

Roll a d20 and add any relevant modifiers.

Your opponent does the same.

Whoever has the largest result wins.

If both results are the same, nothing happens most of the time.

Advancement

Experience

As a guideline, characters receive 1 xp for a low-risk accomplishment, 2 xp for a moderate-risk accomplishment, and 4 xp for a high-risk accomplishment.

Characters can also receive xp from various Downtime activities.
