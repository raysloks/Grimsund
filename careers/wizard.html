Wizard

Practitioner of arcane magics.

Arcane Focus

Most wizards require an arcane focus, a source of magical power, to cast spells.

An arcane focus can be virtually any object, but generally look ancient or strange.

Features

  • Tell whether an item is magical or not by touching it.
  • Sense the location of spells being cast within half a mile.
