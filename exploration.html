Tests

If a player attempts an action where the outcome is not certain, the referee should ask the player to make a test.

The referee may specify any stats or skills that might apply to the test. Usually, one stat and one skill should be selected.

The player may then argue that another stat or skill would be more applicable in the current situation.

Note that players should never ask to make a test; the referee is almost always the one to call for a test.

However, if a player thinks the referee missed calling for a test, they are encouraged to speak up. This shouldn't happen too often, of course.
